## Overview

Patrick Fay describes how NVIDIA AI Blueprints function as reference architectures for enterprise AI implementation. These packages contain pre-built microservices, model serving capabilities, and data pipeline tools designed to accelerate AI development.

However, Blueprints alone lack essential enterprise features needed for production deployment. IBM Fusion provides the missing infrastructure layer—"the platform for building, bringing, and running enterprise AI applications."

## Key Points

**What Blueprints Provide:**
- GPU-accelerated reference architectures
- NVIDIA NIM microservices
- Data ingest and curation pipelines
- Example agentic logic

**What IBM Fusion Adds:**
The collaboration delivers enterprise-grade capabilities including governance, identity controls, data sovereignty, lifecycle automation, high availability, and disaster recovery features.

**Validated Solutions:**
Two blueprints are currently validated on IBM Fusion:
- **AI-Q**: A research blueprint for document analysis, already deployed as a medical research assistant
- **VSS** (Visual Search and Summarization): A multimodal tool for visual inspection and incident analysis from video feeds

The partnership will introduce additional industry-specific blueprints throughout the coming year, with representatives discussing the integration at NVIDIA GTC on March 16th.
